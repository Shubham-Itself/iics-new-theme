about us

Where Imagination meets Innovation

Welcome to the Indian Institute of Creative Skills (IICS), an institution affiliated with NSDC Academy and operated by the Media & Entertainment Skills Council (MESC), funded by NSDC, functions under the aegis of the Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (MSDE).

At IICS, we’re redefining creative education in India. Backed by industry leaders and powered by real-world learning, our mission is to equip the next generation of storytellers, creators, and innovators for success in the dynamic world of Media & Entertainment.

Admission Process

Know More

1. Online Entrance Exam

  • Tailored to your program
  • Tests creativity, aptitude, general awareness & industry interest
  • Conducted remotely and monitored

2. Portfolio Review / Audition

  • Creative Programs: Your portfolio shall be reviewed to assess your artistic vision, originality, and skill.
  • Performance Programs: You’ll be invited to submit or perform an audition task to showcase your talent.

3. Personal Interview

  • Shortlisted applicants will go through a one-on-one interview round (online or in-person).

4. Final Evaluation & Selection

  • Your performance across all stages — entrance test, portfolio/audition, and interview — is considered for final selection.
  • Selected students will receive a formal Offer Letter with details on admission, fee, and joining formalities.

Student Life

Life at IICS is all about exploring your creativity while building real skills that matter. From hands-on projects to fun, collaborative classrooms, students learn by doing—whether it’s designing a game, creating animations, or working on digital media. You’ll meet like-minded people, get guidance from mentors, and grow at your own pace. It's a space where your ideas are heard, and your passion turns into a career path. At IICS, we keep it real, practical, and focused on you.
