About us

At the Indian Institute of Creative Skills (IICS), creativity isn’t just taught — it’s lived, explored, and celebrated every day. We’re not your typical classroom. We’re a vibrant space where ideas come alive, where passion meets purpose, and where the next generation of storytellers, performers, designers, and innovators begin their journeys.

Here, you’ll find more than just a campus — you’ll find a launchpad. Backed by the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) and powered by the Media & Entertainment Skills Council (MESC), IICS is built for those who dream big and dare to be different. Whether you're into filmmaking, music, gaming, or the performing arts, you’ll be immersed in a world that’s as dynamic as the industries you’ll be entering.

We believe real learning happens when you’re doing, creating, collaborating — and sometimes even failing forward. That’s why our programs are hands-on, industry-driven, and designed to help you grow not just as a professional, but as a creative thinker and a confident individual. With mentorship from industry experts, cutting-edge technology at your fingertips, and a community that thrives on curiosity and collaboration, you’ll be empowered to shape the future of India’s creative economy — and your own future, too.

At IICS, we don't just prepare you for a job — we prepare you to lead, to innovate, and to own your story.

Welcome to a place where your imagination has no limits.

Our Vision

At the Indian Institute of Creative Skills, we believe in the power of creativity to shape the future. Our vision is to help India shine on the global stage by nurturing a new wave of talented artists, storytellers, and creative professionals who are ready to lead the Media & Entertainment industry—here at home and around the world.

If you're ready to create, lead, and inspire—this is where your journey begins.

Our Mission

At the Indian Institute of Creative Skills, our mission is simple — to turn your passion into a profession.

We believe talent deserves opportunity. That’s why our programs are built to bridge that gap — with training designed by industry experts, mentorship from award-winning professionals, and a focus on innovation, entrepreneurship, and original content creation.

While we keep our eyes on global standards, we proudly stay rooted in India’s rich creative culture. Whether you're dreaming of directing films, designing games, crafting stories, or building brands — we’re here to help you make it real.

Let’s build the future of media and entertainment, together.

Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship

MSDE Is Responsible For Coordination Of All Skill Development Efforts Across The Country, Removal Of Disconnect Between Demand And Supply Of Skilled Manpower, Building The Vocational And Technical Training Framework, Skill Up-Gradation, Building Of New Skills And Innovative Thinking Not Only For Existing Jobs But Also Jobs That Are To Be Created.

Skill India Mission

Launched in 2015, the Skill India Mission is a flagship initiative under MSDE aimed at training over 40 crore Indians in various industry-relevant skills by 2025. IICS is proud to contribute to this mission by creating high-impact learning experiences that turn raw talent into employable, entrepreneurial, and industry-ready professionals in the creative sector.

National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC)

National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) as the ‘Principal Architect of the Skill Ecosystem’, stands as India’s leading force in skilling, reskilling, and upskilling, driving transformative initiatives that unlock opportunities for the workforce of tomorrow. By providing funding support, concessional loans, and innovative financial solutions, NSDC empowers enterprises, start-ups, and organizations to make a meaningful impact in emerging and futuristic skill sectors and empower India’s workforce to compete Globally.

Media & Entertainment Skills Council (MESC)

The Media and Entertainment Skills Council (MESC), established in 2012, is a Not-for-Profit Organization under the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, dedicated to enhancing skill development in the Media and Entertainment sector. Initially funded by the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) and incubated at the Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry (FICCI), MESC operates as an awarding body with the National Council for Vocational and Education Trainings (NCVET).
